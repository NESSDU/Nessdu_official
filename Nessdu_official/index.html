About NESSDU

The North East Students’ Society, Delhi University (NESSDU) is an official student representative body working for the welfare, safety, dignity, and rights of students from the North Eastern states studying at the University of Delhi.

Vision

To ensure a safe, inclusive, and respectful academic environment for every North East student.

Mission

  • Protection of rights and dignity
  • Response to discrimination and violence
  • Institutional and legal support
  • Promotion of unity and leadership
